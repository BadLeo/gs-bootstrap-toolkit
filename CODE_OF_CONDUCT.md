# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, professional collaboration. Participants should:

- communicate constructively
- assume good intent
- focus criticism on the work, not the person
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or abusive behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- personal attacks or insults
- discriminatory language or conduct
- sustained disruptive behavior
- publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not meet these standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer through the repository contact channels or security contact process documented in `SECURITY.md`.
